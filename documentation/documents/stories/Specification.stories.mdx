- **Ethernet Ports:** 1 WAN, 1 LAN for wired network connections
- **USB Ports:** 1 USB 2.0 port for peripherals and expansions
- **Power Input:** Micro USB port for easy and universal charging
- **Buttons:** Reset and toggle buttons for simple device management
- **LED Indicators:** Includes indicators for Power, Wi-Fi, and more to provide status information at a glance
